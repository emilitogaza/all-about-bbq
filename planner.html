Chapter 10 — The Tool

🗓️ The Cook Planner

The most common way to ruin a barbecue is arithmetic. Pick your cut, your weight, and when you want to eat — and get the whole cook, planned backwards, with the hold already built in.

10.1Plan the cook

Estimates follow the course's methods (chapters 2–7) with a safety margin — barbecue finishes when it finishes, which is exactly why the hold exists: an early brisket rests happily for hours, a late one ruins dinner. When in doubt, start earlier and hold longer.
